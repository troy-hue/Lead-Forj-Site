# Lead Forj Website

## About Lead Forj
Lead Forj is a signal-driven cold email agency founded by Troy Meadows. We build AI-augmented email and LinkedIn outreach systems for B2B service firms (consultants, business coaches, niche agency owners).

## Brand Voice
- Calm, direct, outcome-focused
- Professional but warm
- Confident without being pushy
- Conversational, not corporate
- Brief. Every sentence earns its place.

## Core Differentiators
- **Signal-driven:** Only reach out when there's a real reason (job change, company growth, hiring signal, product launch, market shift)
- **Reputation-first:** Optimize for quality conversations, not inbox saturation. Zero deliverability incidents.
- **Systems, not campaigns:** Infrastructure that compounds over time, not one-off blasts
- **AI-augmented:** AI handles research and pattern recognition. Humans define voice, intent, and judgment.

## Core Beliefs
- We optimize for signal, not scale.
- The future of email is fewer emails with better signal.
- We use AI and automation to support deep prospect research, not mass sending.
- We protect deliverability by design.
- We book higher-quality conversations by being selective.
